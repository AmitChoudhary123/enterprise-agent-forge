# Business Case

## Objective

Provide a reusable reference pattern for enterprise-safe agentic AI workflows.

## Problem

Enterprises are experimenting with agents, but many pilots lack the operating controls required for production: approval gates, auditability, risk classification, rollback planning, and business ownership.

## Stakeholders

- CIO / CTO / CDO
- AI transformation leaders
- Enterprise architects
- Risk and compliance leaders
- AI engineering and platform teams
- Business operations owners

## Value proposition

Enterprise Agent Forge helps teams prototype agentic workflows with governance built in from the start. It creates a shared artifact that technical teams, business leaders, and risk stakeholders can inspect together.

## Expected impact

- Faster alignment between AI engineering and governance teams
- Better agent use-case qualification before pilot spend
- Lower risk of uncontrolled automation
- More credible executive conversations about agentic AI readiness
- Reusable patterns for policy, audit, approval, and scenario design
